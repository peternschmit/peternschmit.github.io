Peter Schmit

Peter Schmit is a composer and multimedia artist from Lincoln, Nebraska. He is currently studying Emerging Media Arts, with minors in Music Technology and Software Development, at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

Civics Scribble

Caleb Kirilov and I created the music and sound for Nebraska Public Media's educational line-drawing game, Civics Scribble.

Memorial Stadium Virtual Tour

A virtual tour Kayla Solorzano and I assembled for Nebraska Public Media Labs. We did the bulk of the work in 3DVista Virtual Tour Pro, using a series of panoramic images rendered in Unreal Engine, from a 1:1 scale model of the stadium modeled by Steve Kolbe and Kayla LaPoure for the stadium's 100th anniversary.

Headbang Hero

A headtracking-based rhythm game mixed with simple mechatronics, created in Fall 2023.

A head-tracking video game controller created using Arduino with a 9-axis IMU and a 3D-printed headphone mount. Shoulder creature printed from a LIDAR scan of myself, and controlled with a servo. Music composed in Logic Pro. Game made with Unity, using the DryWetMIDI plugin.

bicycle

An installation at EMA Open Studios in Spring 2023.

An interactive installation combining physical elements, video projection, and music. Audiences control the playback speed and direction of an ambient music piece using bike pedals and a potentiometer, respectively.

I created the ambient music (listen below) in Logic Pro using a seven-string guitar in an altered tuning. I filmed the footage in my backyard, and programmed the interactions in the Arduino IDE and p5.js.
